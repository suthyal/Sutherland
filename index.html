Alex Sutherland

My passion lies in understanding the world using mathematical tools and I specialize in the theory of ocean waves. I have experience running experiments to generate data in the largest wave basin in North America, and I have extensive knowledge of instrumentation and data analysis in order to validate physical models with those experimental results. I have written about much of the physics of waves, experimentation and data analysis process in great detail and enjoy sharing my understanding with students and other researchers. I am currently a graduate student at Oregon State University planning on graduating with my MSc. in Mathematics in Fall of 2024, and with my PhD. in Civil Engineering in Spring 2025.
